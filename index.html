About Us

Who are we?

Eng-India aims at removing the fear of spoken English and public speaking that dwells in people. Job interviews, group discussions and public gatherings, there are so many areas where we have to speak flawless English but due to various reasons, we aren't able to do that. We are here to cater for that confidence and believe that you can speak as smoothly as any professional orator. We improve your verbatium, give you plenty of exercises and instill in you the sheer confidence that you need. Thankyou. We are extremely proud of what we do. 200+ students learned to speak English fluently like a professional and are excelling in their jobs today as you read this post. We are proud because we made a difference

Our levels of Teaching

These are the basic levels in which we make you go higher!

Level One

We ensure that your existing language is refined by correcting your everyday grammatical fallacies. In this level, not just your language is refined but we efficiently enhance the confidence that it takes to come across the self realisation that it's just a matter of time when you'll speak like a trained professional.

Level Two

This is the level where we add weight and beauty to your words and presentation by training you in psychological skills, presentation hacks. From the very basics to the most complex delivery skills, we have it all covered. We basically aim at creating a personality that people willing want to listen to.

Level Three

The place where magic happens is Level 3. We get you involved in Group Discussions, personal interviews. You get to tackle some very feared speakers from all around the globe. Yes! You heard it right. And Guess what, quite often our students at Eng India beat them.
